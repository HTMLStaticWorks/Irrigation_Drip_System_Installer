System Care

Follow these best practices to ensure your drip system operates reliably for years.

Regular Flushing

Flush lateral drip lines and mainlines every 2-4 weeks to clear out any silt or organic accumulation before it settles.

Filter Inspections

Clean screen or disc filters regularly. A clogged filter drops system pressure, causing uneven water distribution.

Winterization

Before freezing temperatures arrive, drain all manifold valves, backflow preventers, and blow out lines to prevent crack damage.

Crop Compatibility

Drip systems can be customized to optimize watering for specific crop profiles.

Orchards & Vineyards

Wide spacing emitters target individual tree roots, improving fruit sizing and sugar levels while controlling weeds between rows.

Vegetables & Row Crops

Thin-wall drip tapes deliver uniform, frequent watering along rows, maximizing marketability and uniform growth.

Greenhouses & Nurseries

Micro-drip setups provide precise control for individual pots or flats, integrating easily with fertigation and automated sensors.
